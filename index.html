About Me

Hello! I'm Billy Connors, a former teacher and now a Computer Game Engineering MSc student passionate about learning how to improve my technical skills and making a contribution to the creative medium that I love.
As a programmer, I primarily use C++ but I have experience building projects in C#, Lua, Java, Python, and Haskell. I'm experienced in building projects with the largest game engines, particularly Unreal and Unity, but also enjoy learning to use smaller engines and frameworks such as Godot, Pico-8, and raylib.
In addition to working on my technical skills, I take every project as an opportunity to work on my design skills. Game design has fascinated me all my life and I find the creative experience of creating a game to be particularly fulfilling. To see my work or contact me, please see below.
